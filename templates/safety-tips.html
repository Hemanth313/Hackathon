Safety Tips

General Safety

Walking Alone

  • Stay in well-lit areas at night
  • Keep your phone charged and accessible
  • Walk confidently and be aware of your surroundings

Transportation

  • Check cab details before entering (number, driver photo)
  • Share ride details with trusted contacts
  • Sit behind the driver in cabs

Emergency Preparedness

Phone Setup

  • Program emergency numbers into speed dial
  • Enable emergency SOS features on your phone
  • Keep location services enabled

Public Situations

  • Create a scene if harassed - shout, draw attention
  • Identify safe public places (police stations, hospitals)
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, leave

Technology Safety

Digital Security

  • Turn off location sharing in social media posts
  • Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts
  • Enable two-factor authentication

App Safety

  • Only download apps from official stores
  • Review app permissions carefully
  • Log out of shared devices
